Guide for Operators

  1. Begin at Beddington Asda.
  2. At the petrol station next to the Asda parking lot, turn right behind the Asda building.
  3. At the junction, turn right.
  4. At the traffic lights, turn right.
  5. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across.
  6. At the next traffic lights, turn left into Courtney Road.
  7. After going down the hill and reaching traffic lights, turn left.
  8. At the mini roundabout, take the 2nd exit (right turn).
  9. At the traffic lights, stick to the left, going straight across the motorway and into the exit.
  10. In the exit, turn right towards Centrale.
  11. At the roundabout, go over the dual tram tracks and yellow road markings towards Centrale.
  12. At the traffic lights following Centrale, turn right, then pause at Poplar Walk.
  13. After pausing at Poplar Walk, turn right at the traffic lights.
  14. At the traffic lights, drive straight across. Then, pull into the bus lane and stay there. It should lead you down a small road.
  15. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit into Dingwall Road.
  16. Take a left turn after driving across the dual tram tracks.
  17. Pull into East Croydon Bus Station Stand E2 and pause.
Return journey
  1. Pull out of stand E2, and head up the hill avoiding stands E6 and E7.
  2. At the traffic lights, turn right crossing the dual tram tracks.
  3. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit.
  4. At the traffic lights, turn left, then take a slight left. If navigated correctly, you should be on a single lane road to the right of a single tram track.
  5. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across into Fairfield Halls (red road).
  6. At the large roundabout, follow road markings for 'A212', then started following the road markings for 'A232'. This should be the 3rd exit. If navigated correctly, you should be on a motorway with an exit straight to your left, but do not go down that exit.
  7. Take a left turn down the exit once you see traffic cones in front of you.
  8. At the large roundabout with a motorway running through the middle, take the 3rd exit.
  9. At the traffic lights, take the exit to your left.
  10. At the mini roundabout, take the 1st exit (left turn).
  11. At the traffic lights, turn right up the steep hill into Courtney Road.
  12. At the next traffic lights, turn right.
  13. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across.
  14. At the next traffic lights, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  15. At the junction, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  16. Turn left into Beddington Asda and pause.
