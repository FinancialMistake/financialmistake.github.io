Guide for operåtors

  1. Depart Beddington Asda.
  2. At the petrol station, keep left, then take the 2nd exit (right) at the mini roundabout.
  3. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit, then proceed straight.
  4. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit.
  5. At the long roundabout, take the 2nd exit by driving by Ampere Way Tram Stop, skipping the small exit into the car park, and then taking the next exit, the one that is right of a large parking lot. Pause at the bus stop.
Return journey
  1. Depart IKEA, then do a U-turn at the roundabout by taking the 4th exit.
  2. Keep left by taking the very next exit on the roundabout, then proceed straight.
  3. At the roundabout, take the 2nd exit, then proceed straight.
  4. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit, then proceed straight.
  5. Turn left into Beddington Asda, then pause.
