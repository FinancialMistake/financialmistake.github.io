Guide for Operators

  1. Begin at Beddington Asda.
  2. At the petrol station next to the Asda parking lot, turn right behind the Asda building.
  3. At the junction, turn right.
  4. At the traffic lights, turn right, then immediately left towards Waddon Marsh.
  5. After Waddon Marsh Tram Stop, turn right.
  6. Pause at Waddon Marsh.
  7. After pausing, turn left at the traffic lights.
  8. At the next traffic lights, turn left into Courtney Road.
  9. After going down the hill and reaching traffic lights, turn left.
  10. At the mini roundabout, take the 2nd exit (right turn).
  11. At the traffic lights, stick to the left, going straight across the motorway and into the exit.
  12. In the exit, turn right towards Centrale.
  13. At the roundabout, go over the dual tram tracks and yellow road markings towards Centrale.
  14. At the traffic lights following Centrale, drive straight across.
  15. Turn right into West Croydon Bus Station pulling into stand B2, then pausing.
Return journey
  1. Turn left out of West Croydon Bus Station.
  2. At the traffic lights, drive straight across.
  3. Upon reaching Centrale, stick to the left and drive under the large grey building.
  4. After continuing straight and crossing the single tram track, pause at Waddon Marsh Tram Stop.
  5. After departing Church Street Tram Stop, go straight to the traffic lights in front of you, sticking to the left. If navigated correctly, you should be driving on a road with dual tram tracks to the right of a motorway going upwards with Reeves Corner Tram Stop to your right.
  6. At the mini roundabout, take the 2nd exit (straight across).
  7. At the traffic lights, turn right up the steep hill into Courtney Road.
  8. At the next traffic lights, turn right.
  9. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across.
  10. At the next traffic lights, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  11. At the junction, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  12. Turn left into Beddington Asda and pause.
