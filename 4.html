Guide for Operators

  1. Begin at Beddington Asda.
  2. At the petrol station next to the Asda parking lot, keep left, then take the 2nd exit at the mini roundabout (left turn).
  3. At the roundabout next to the Beddington Lane bus depot, take the 3rd exit.
  4. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit.
  5. Keep going straight avoiding the exit upwards.
  6. After you have merged onto the dual carriageway and reaching traffic lights, drive straight ahead driving past Waddon Marsh Sainsbury's.
  7. At the next traffic lights, drive straight ahead.
  8. At the next traffic lights, turn left into Courtney Road.
  9. After going down the hill and reaching traffic lights, turn left.
  10. At the mini roundabout, take the 2nd exit (right turn).
  11. At the traffic lights, stick to the same lane, turning right onto the dual carriageway, then, continue driving straight.
  12. At the large roundabout, take the 2nd exit.
  13. At the mini roundabout, take the 1st exit into South Croydon.
  14. At the intersection with traffic lights, drive straight across.
  15. At the traffic lights, turn right.
  16. Turn right into Aberdeen Road.
  17. At the small intersection with Heathfield Road to the left and right, drive straight across.
  18. At the traffic lights, turn left.
  19. At the next traffic lights right after the Swan and Sugar Loaf bus stop, drive straight across.
  20. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across.
  21. Upon reaching South Croydon Bus Garage, you may pause.
Return journey
  1. Do a 'U' turn using the garage, and proceed straight.
  2. At the traffic lights, turn left into Nottingham Road.
  3. At the small intersection, turn right into Warham Road.
  4. At the traffic lights, turn left onto the main road.
  5. At the traffic lights right after Swan and Sugar Loaf, take a slight left turn.
  6. At the mini roundabout, take the 1st exit (straight across).
  7. At the large roundabout after the mini roundabout, take the 2nd exit.
  8. At the traffic lights, make a slight left turn.
  9. At the mini roundabout, take the 1st exit (left turn).
  10. At the traffic lights, turn right up the steep hill into Courtney Road.
  11. At the next traffic lights, turn right.
  12. At the next traffic lights, drive straight across.
  13. At the next traffic lights, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  14. At the junction, turn left towards Beddington Asda.
  15. Turn left into Beddington Asda and pause.
