News

Applications closed!

1 SEPTEMBER 2025

The applications for the rally have now closed!
We finished with 8 applicants and are ready for the rally on the date!
See you there!!

More participants!

20 AUGUST 2025

We've had 4 participants for a little while but they've risen to 7! A timetable has already been prepared for service 1 with more to come!

Route guides are back!

6 AUGUST 2025

At the last rally, we took away route guides from routes. That caused some confusion at the last rally, so route guides are back!
They've also all been rewritten, no longer taken from previous rallies.

Applications open!

5 AUGUST 2025

The applications for the autumn 2025 rally have opened!
This starts yet another exciting season for the Beddington Bus Rally, and we're excited to see it unfold!
The applications will be closing on the 1st of September in the late evening so don't miss your chance to apply!
If you're too late, you can still be on display.
See you at the rally folks!
